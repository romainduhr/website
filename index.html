About me

Romain / 21 years / Lille, France

Since I was little, I have always been passionate about computer science and electronics. I followed tutorials and other enriching content to learn programming. I even took great pride in being able to assemble my own PC from components at the age of 12. That's why, after a particular year (due to COVID-19), during which I discovered The Coding Train channel, enabling me to apply my skills in various algorithms, I decided to pursue a 2-year integrated preparatory program at Polytech. This allowed me to strengthen my theoretical knowledge in engineering domains. I also had the opportunity to explore languages such as Python, HTML, and CSS.

Later on, I chose to specialize in embedded systems, particularly in IoT. Juggling part-time jobs and classes, I developed a proactive lifestyle and remain motivated in my endeavors. Over the past two years, I have learned more about microcontroller programming using languages like C, assembly, and VHDL. Additionally, I delved into object-oriented programming in Java.

Finally , I love looking for enriching content. I am self-learner and passionate about the field of computer science. Alongside my studies, I have acquired some web development skills, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
